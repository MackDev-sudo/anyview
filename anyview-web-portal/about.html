Analytic Web Portal for Automated DC Infrastructure Management

AnyView is an advanced web portal designed to automate and streamline data center (DC) infrastructure management processes. It offers comprehensive solutions for tasks such as builds, zoning, storage allocation, and expansion, tailored to meet the unique requirements of each client.

Key Features

Automated Processes

AnyView automates critical DC operations including server builds, zoning configurations, and storage management. This automation reduces manual intervention, enhances operational efficiency, and minimizes errors.

Customizability

Clients can customize AnyView to align with specific operational needs. Whether adjusting workflows, integrating new technologies, or implementing unique business rules, AnyView adapts effortlessly.

Data Integration

AnyView gathers data from various sources such as storage systems, backup solutions, and Brocade switches. It consolidates this information into a unified dashboard, providing holistic insights and facilitating informed decision-making.

User-Friendly Interface

Featuring an intuitive interface, AnyView presents complex data in a clear and user-friendly manner. Real-time updates and customizable dashboards empower users with actionable insights at their fingertips.

Analytics and Reporting

AnyView offers advanced analytics capabilities to analyze DC performance metrics, utilization trends, and compliance status. Customizable reports enable proactive management and strategic planning.

Security and Compliance

With robust security measures, AnyView ensures data integrity and confidentiality. It complies with industry standards and regulatory requirements, safeguarding sensitive information effectively.

Benefits

Enhanced Efficiency

Automates routine tasks, reducing operational time and resources.

Improved Visibility

Centralizes data from diverse sources for comprehensive oversight and decision-making.

Cost Optimization

Reduces operational costs associated with manual processes and errors.

Scalability

Scales effortlessly to accommodate growing infrastructure needs and business expansion.

Risk Mitigation

Minimizes risks related to manual errors and non-compliance through automated processes and robust security measures.
